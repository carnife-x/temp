Data Efficient Learning with Less Data State of the art AI and Deep Learning are very data hungry. This comes at significant cost including larger resource costs (multiple expensive GPUs and cloud costs), training times (often times multiple days), and human labeling costs and time. Decile attempts to solve this by answering the following question. Can we train state of the art deep models with only a sample (say 5 to 10\%) of massive datasets, while having neglibible impact in accuracy? Can we do this while reducing training time/cost by an order of magnitude, and/or significantly reducing the amount of labeled data required? Decile provides two major paradigms for this: CORDS (reducing training time and cost), DISTIL (reducing labeling cost and time).

Modules

CORDS

DISTIL

Reduce Labeling costs via Interactive Learning
